30 Seconds of Every Monster Hunter World Weapon

MHW 30 Seconds Every Weapon

There it is, the video I've been working on for the last few months. Some of these clips took me an absurd amount of resets. I remember around three years ago, when I released my "Thank you Iceborne" video, I was in a rush because Rise was coming out, and I posted it just the day before Rise. Now that I've returned to World Iceborne and gained much more experience both as a player and as an editor, I can finally give this game the tribute it deserves.
