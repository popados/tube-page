DnB Japanese Drum and Bass

I aim to create a unique DnB that incorporates Japanese elements, so it may differ from traditional DnB.''

However, I put my heart into making it, so I hope you can enjoy my music with an open mind☺️

I'm excited to introduce a new subgenre I created: Zen DnB

Features: Use of traditional Japanese instruments: Melodies centered around instruments like the shamisen and koto. Incorporation of other traditional Japanese instruments such as the shakuhachi.

Melodious and soothing sound: Fusion of deep bass and drum elements with the essence of Japanese harmony.

Complex breakbeats: Maintaining traditional jungle and drum and bass breakbeats while harmonizing with Japanese instruments.

BPM is around 160-180 (with some exceptions). Please enjoy my new musical style, "Zen DnB"! I hope you'll join me in nurturing this genre together🥰
